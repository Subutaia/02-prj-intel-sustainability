1968

Intel Founded

Intel is founded by Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore, starting a legacy of innovation.

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel launches the 4004, the world's first commercial microprocessor.

1978

8086 Processor

The 8086 processor introduces the x86 architecture, powering future computers.

1985

386 Processor

Intel introduces the 386 processor with 32-bit architecture, ushering in a new era of performance and multitasking for personal computers.
